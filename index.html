I am delighted to introduce myself as the Acting Head teacher of St Patrick's RC Primary School, which serves the Catholic community of Newport.

We are committed to working for all children in partnership with parents, to make our school a happy and dynamic place, where every child is able to enjoy their learning, and succeed in their education.
